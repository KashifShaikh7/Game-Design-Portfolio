Money Rush

Endless Runner

This is my personal Subway Surfer-inspired endless runner, set in a post-apocalyptic world. Instead of running on railway tracks, the player sprints down an abandoned road, dodging cars and collecting coins. I handled character modeling, texturing, rigging, and level design, while using some ready-made assets for buildings and vehicles to speed up development.

The Logic :
Some years ago, I mostly wondered. That, how far can even subway surfer runner can go? And when I started making this, I realized we never have to move our runner. It just have to jump, and slide. So I just added the run animation to my character and moved the platforms/levels towards the player, to give a nice running effect. This worked smoothly. The levels spawn in front of player, gets past the player and destroys.
