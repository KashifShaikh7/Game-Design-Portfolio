Frog Jump

Endless Platformer

screenshot
I was really Inpired by the game - Dunk Shot. The Idea was simple, the player have to jump on platforms that spawn in front, by holding tap to charge power. The players have to take quick action to land on the next platform. The Level keeps on getting harder an player progresses, introducing new platforms like moving logs and traps.

Fruit Trouble

Casual

screenshot
This is a game similar to one I used to play years ago. Maybe you can guess. It's was one of the flash games called Bubble Trouble. The player clicks to fire knifes, if it hits the fruit, the fruit gets divided in smaller fruits. this happenes until its cherry and destroying it no more divides. If any fruit hits the knife, the game is over (ya, I know it makes no sense). The difficulty in this game is made by the player itself in order to complete level.

Dark Slayer

VR Action

me playing my game
So the main task was to make a game like beat saber. And I took this spooky theme. So there is a spawner in front of player which spawn skulls after a delay. We have to kill these monsters with out cursed sword. If you miss some skulls the game is over. I learned a lot of things while playing other VR games too, like the labs - long bow, covert syndrome. I came to know that. Player physical movement is the key (mostly hands). And hence I loved to play long bow, which made my hands feel pain of using a bow.
