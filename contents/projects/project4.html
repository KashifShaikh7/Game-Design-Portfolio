Pumpkin Shooter

AR Game

This is my first ever AR Game made in Unity using Vuforia. The game is simple - scan the target, the timer begins with random pumpkins and bombs spawning. tap the pumpkin to get score. and loose score if tapped on bomb or missed a pumpkin.
